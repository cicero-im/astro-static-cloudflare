- Astro 6 static output, deployed to Cloudflare Workers via `[assets]`.
- Inlang/Paraglide i18n with locale-prefixed routes.
- Sitemap, robots, RSS, OG images, and JSON-LD baked in.
- Vite+ for typed lint/format/check (`vp check`).
